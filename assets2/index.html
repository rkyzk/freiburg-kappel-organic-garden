Welcome!

Come out in our beautiful field! Located in Freiburg Kappel, we grow gorgeous flowers organically. From March to October we have a variety of flowers: Daffodils, Tulips, Dahlias, Gladiolus, Sunflowers, Chrysanthemums and more. You can come by anytime, take a walk, pick flowers to take home if you want—if you pick some flowers, don’t forget to make payments in the box located at the entrance.
